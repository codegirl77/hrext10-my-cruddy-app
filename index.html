Pocket Pups!

A website for you to create and take care of virtual dogs/puppies!

How to Create a Dog

Step One: Choose Your Dog
Step Two: Give Your Dog a Name
Step Three: Give Your Dog a Personality Type
Step Four: Click the Create Dog Button
Step Five: Find Your Newly Created Dog in Your Dogs Section
Things to Note:

To update or to delete a dog, type in the dog's name and click the update or delete button. Click the clear all dogs button to remove all of your dogs.

How to Create a Puppy

Step One: Click on the Images of Two of Your Dogs to Make Them Parents
Step Two: Type in the Name You Want Your Puppy to Have
Step Three: Click the Create Puppy Button

Your Dogs

Below are your happy pups! Click the buttons on each of your dogs to feed them, help them nap, and to play with them.
